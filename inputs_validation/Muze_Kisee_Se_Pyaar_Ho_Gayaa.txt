Khul gayaa fir bhee dil kaa fasaanaa  
Dil ne ye kyaa kiyaa o salone piyaa moraa dhadake jiyaa  
O o o o muze kisee se pyaar ho gayaa

Aaur jo main kahanaa chaahaa dil mein hee rah gayaa  
O o o o muze kisee se pyaar ho gayaa
